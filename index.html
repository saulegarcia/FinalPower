Welcome to North America's and soon to be the world's best gym!

From our humble beginnings as one small gym in a humble basement studio on Staten Island, New York to accross hundreds of cities in the eastern hemisphere. We had one goal. have everyone work hard, so they can play hard. In 1997, we made our business not only a home, but as a welcoming place to get fit for a diverse group of people. Though we understand the importance of fitness to improve people's lives, we also know that exercise is hard work and that everybody can use a bit more ambition to achieve their goals. So we've combined health and entertainment so we can have fun doing intense exercise.

  • all day basketball and racquetball courts
  • swimming pools and zumba classes
  • a great variety of cardio and free weights!
